### FAQ:
Some Environments are only tested with the Quest 2. Use at your own risk on Quest 1.
	
With Quest firmware v25 there is a bug that results in a black void or *ShellEnv* crashes.

Uninstall or install  [this](https://sidequestvr.com/app/3889/custom-home-canyon-enviroment-panorama-360-image) environment and reboot your Quest to fix this issue.

When you get this error: "INSTALL_FAILED_UPDATE_INCOMPATIBLE" [uninstall the original environment](https://github.com/Quest-Homes/Home-Environments/blob/main/Uninstall.jpg) and try again.
